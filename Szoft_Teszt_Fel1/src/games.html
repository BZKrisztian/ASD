Most famous Souls-likes

Demon Souls

The one that started it all

In the haunting kingdom of Boletaria, dark forces have unleashed chaos, plunging the realm into despair. As a hero chosen by fate, you must venture forth to confront the demonic threats that roam the land. Through perilous encounters and unraveling mysteries, you'll uncover the tragic history of Boletaria and the sinister powers that threaten its very existence.


Dark Souls

The beginning of the genre

The players journey through the bleak and decaying kingdom of Lordran, a world on the brink of oblivion. As the Undead, cursed with eternal resurrection, they navigate treacherous landscapes and face formidable adversaries, both human and monstrous. Amidst the crumbling ruins and forsaken fortresses, they unravel the enigmatic lore of the world, piecing together fragments of a dark and complex history. Their quest is one of survival, exploration, and ultimately, the pursuit of destiny as they confront the gods themselves in a battle to decide the fate of Lordran.


Dark Souls 2

Prepare to Die

The players find themselves in the forsaken kingdom of Drangleic, a land plagued by a curse that binds its inhabitants to the cycle of death and rebirth. As an Undead seeking a cure for their affliction, they embark on a perilous journey across treacherous landscapes and encounter a multitude of adversaries, both monstrous and human. Amidst the shadows of ancient ruins and forgotten kingdoms, they uncover the truth behind the curse and the dark secrets that shroud Drangleic. Their quest for redemption and salvation leads them to confront powerful foes and make crucial decisions that shape the fate of the kingdom and its people.


Dark Souls 3

The Fire Fades

The players delve into a world on the brink of apocalypse, where the fire fades, and darkness threatens to consume everything. As the Ashen One, risen from the grave, they embark on a quest to link the flame and stave off the encroaching darkness. Throughout their journey across desolate landscapes and crumbling kingdoms, they encounter fierce adversaries and unravel the intricate lore of the world, haunted by echoes of past cycles. Guided by enigmatic beings and driven by a sense of duty, they must confront powerful enemies and make profound choices that will determine the fate of the world and the legacy of those who came before.


Bloodborne

The players find themselves in the cursed city of Yharnam, where a mysterious illness spreads through its streets, transforming citizens into grotesque beasts. As a Hunter, they delve into the dark alleys and Gothic architecture of the city, uncovering its sinister secrets and the truth behind the plague. Guided by cryptic messages and confronting eldritch horrors, they must navigate through the madness that grips Yharnam while facing formidable foes and unraveling the cosmic mysteries that lie at the heart of the city's downfall.


Sekiro: Shadows Die Twice

players assume the role of a skilled shinobi known as Sekiro, the "One-Armed Wolf." Set in the late 1500s Sengoku period in Japan, the game follows Sekiro's quest for revenge against a samurai who kidnapped his lord and severed his arm. Armed with a katana and a prosthetic shinobi arm imbued with various tools and abilities, players navigate through the war-torn land of Ashina, engaging in intense sword combat and stealthy assassination missions. As Sekiro, they must confront powerful enemies, including otherworldly creatures, and uncover the dark secrets of the Ashina clan while mastering the game's unique posture-based combat system.


Elden Ring

The players find themlseves in the fantastical world of the Lands Between, a sprawling realm filled with ancient ruins, mythical creatures, and powerful beings. The game follows the journey of the Tarnished, an exiled outcast, as they seek to become an Elden Lord and wield the power of the titular Elden Ring, which has been shattered, plunging the world into chaos. Tasked with reclaiming the fragments of the Elden Ring and defeating formidable adversaries known as demigods, players embark on a vast and perilous adventure across diverse landscapes, encountering allies and enemies alike. As they unravel the mysteries of the Lands Between and confront the darkness that threatens to engulf it, they must harness their strength, courage, and wit to forge their own destiny and restore order to the shattered world.
